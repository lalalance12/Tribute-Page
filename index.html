Linus Torvalds Facts

He is the developer of the linux kernel

Linus Torvalds was born on December 28, 1969

He was born in Helsinki, Finland

Linus Torvalds is a Finnish-American software engineer

Linus Torvalds is the leading supporter of Open Source Software

He also created the distributed version control system Git

He was awarded the C&C Prize by the NEC Corporation in 2010

He was honored with the Millennium Technology Prize in 2012

He was honored with the IEEE Computer Society Computer Pioneer Award in 2014
